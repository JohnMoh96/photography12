Course

The Course will include:

Activities

Workshops

The focus will be based on running several tutorials and workshops
which will mostly include inviting guest speakers from different institutes
The club also is build up with experienced members who will offer more critiques

Field Trips

The club will have outings and field trips as to ensure
every member has enough exposure to field work, and apply concepts done in
tutorials and workshops.

Competitions

There will be competions which will be held, the main objective
is to build photography community, and ensure every member is
ready for field work while also enjoying learning process.
